About Me...

Hello, I am

Tristan James...

I am a passionate artist from San Pablo City, Laguna. I started taking art seriously when I was in junior high school, where I began exploring different techniques, styles, and mediums that helped shape my creativity and artistic identity. Over time, my interest grew beyond traditional drawing and painting and expanded into digital art, allowing me to combine creativity with technology.

Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology at Laguna State Polytechnic University – SPCC. My interests include traditional and digital art, fashion, thrifting, and technology. I enjoy expressing ideas visually through both traditional mediums and digital platforms such as IbisPaint and Adobe Photoshop. Alongside my artistic skills, I have average programming knowledge in Java, C#, HTML, and CSS, which enables me to create and understand technical solutions in the digital space.

My aspiration is to become a solid contributor to the tech industry while maintaining my creativity through art. I aim to integrate innovation and artistic expression, proving that technology and creativity can work hand in hand to build meaningful and impactful solutions
